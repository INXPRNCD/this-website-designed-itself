Before you ask

Fair questions, straight answers.

Will this actually work for my sensitive skin?

It’s the skin type we formulated for first. No fragrance, no essential oils, no drying alcohols, and the serum uses 5% niacinamide — the studied dose, not the stingy one. In our independent 212-person sensitive-skin panel, 96% completed 8 weeks with zero irritation events. And if you’re in the other 4%, the 60-day guarantee exists precisely for you.

What if it just doesn’t work for me?

Then you pay nothing. Email hello@aurel.shop within 60 days of delivery — one line is enough — and we refund the full amount, even if the bottles are empty. No return shipping, no photos of your face, no “have you tried using less?” Average refund time last quarter: 2.8 days.

I have a 7-step routine. Three steps can’t be enough.

For most skin, three well-dosed steps beat seven competing ones — a lot of “sensitivity” is really routine overload. That said, Aurel plays nicely with others: keep your retinoid or vitamin C and slot it between Still and Seal. We’d rather you keep a routine than collect one.

Is it safe during pregnancy?

There are no retinoids, no salicylic acid, and no essential oils anywhere in the line — the usual flagged ingredients simply aren’t in it. Niacinamide, panthenol, squalane and ceramides are generally considered pregnancy-compatible. As always with skincare in pregnancy: show the INCI list (published in full above) to your doctor or midwife.

Is this one of those subscription traps?

No. Everything is a one-time purchase by default. There is an optional refill plan at 15% off — you can skip a delivery, change the rhythm, or cancel it in two clicks from your account page, no chatbot maze, no retention call. We grow by being rebought, not by being hard to leave.

When will I actually see results?

Honest timeline: most people feel less tightness within the first week, see visibly calmer, less blotchy skin around weeks 3–4, and tone improvements by weeks 6–8. In our 8-week consumer study (n=212): 89% reported reduced redness, 93% reported skin feeling “calm or very calm.” If your week 8 doesn’t look like that, the guarantee has you covered.
